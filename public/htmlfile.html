Stages

Stage 1

Start off on the right foot

The goal of the first stage of the incubation program is to help entrepreneurs validate their business idea and determine whether there is a market need for their product or service.

Stage 2

Build the team

During this stage, entrepreneurs will typically conduct market research, identify potential customers, and begin to develop their Minimum Viable Product (MVP) or services. We are willing to bring in tech expertise if required.

Stage 3

Launch time

At this stage, you will receive our support to launch your Minimal Viable Product (MVP) into the market. Once your product is investor ready, we can connect your business to potential funding.
